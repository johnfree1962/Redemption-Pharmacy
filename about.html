About Redemption Pharmacy

Our Story

Redemption Pharmacy was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to bring quality healthcare and affordable medicines to our community. Started as a small neighborhood pharmacy, we've grown to become one of Liberia's most trusted pharmaceutical retailers.

We believe that everyone deserves access to quality medicines and professional healthcare advice. That's why we've invested in the latest technology, trained our staff extensively, and built partnerships with the most reputable pharmaceutical suppliers.

Today, we're proud to serve over 10,000 customers monthly and maintain the highest standards in pharmaceutical care.
